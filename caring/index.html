When a child loses their parents, becoming orphaned, there are no government programs to help that child in any way.  No welfare, no foster care, no assistance or food stamps. 

The child is left alone to beg for food and has very little hope for any type of future.  They are truly the poorest of the poor.

Please join us in caring for children, for these poorest of the poor.  Together we will make a difference one life at a time.
