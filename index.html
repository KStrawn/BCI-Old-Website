Blessing the Children International is a non-profit organization making a difference in the lives of children. Our primary focus is the ministry to orphans and widows in Ethiopia, Africa, called the Adana Children Center – although we partner with more than 30 other ministries and outreaches to varying degrees.

Sponsor an Orphan Child

Founded in 2001 to work with orphans and impoverished children; our passion is ministering to the physical, mental, social, and spiritual needs of children.  Perhaps you would like rescue a child through sponsorship in Ethiopia where people live on less than a dollar a day.

Mission Trips, Internships, & Study Abroad

Perhaps you have considered traveling to a third-world country for a firsthand experience in ministry.  We have many opportunities from a two week mission trip, several internship programs, and even study abroad opportunities.  We invite you to join one of our scheduled teams or to bring your own team.  Either way, you will find the staff of Blessing the Children very committed to ensuring you have a safe and rewarding trip.

If you're looking for an opportunity to make a difference in this world through touching lives, then Blessing the Children is the right choice.  Whether you would like to sponsor a child, support programs helping poor widows and orphans, or visit Ethiopia yourself through one of our many programs, we invite you to join us and get involved today.  You will be glad you did!
